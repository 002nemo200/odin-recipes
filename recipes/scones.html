The Shire Potato Scones

A Timeless classic potato recipe

Scones with a twist as unique as The Shire—made with mashed potatoes, flour, and butter, these scones dance on a griddle for a hobbit-approved texture.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes (boiled and mashed, because who wants a stubborn potato?)
  • Flour (the hobbit's dance floor)
  • Butter (because butter makes everything better)
  • Salt (to season, not for a slippery slope)

Steps

  1. Mix mashed potatoes, flour, and butter in a bowl. Imagine you're making edible Play-Doh for hobbits.
  2. Roll out the dough on a floured surface. Use a rolling pin or your imagination—hobbits are flexible!
  3. Cut the dough into scones. Make them as round as a hobbit's door.
  4. Heat a griddle or skillet. Time for the scones to show off their dance moves!
  5. Cook until both sides are golden brown, like a hobbit basking in the Shire's sunlight.
