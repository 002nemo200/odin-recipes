Hobbit Stuffed Taters

An Evergreen Traditional Dish

Transforming potatoes into a hobbit-approved masterpiece! These spuds take a dip in the mushroom-onion hot tub, only to emerge draped in a cheesy robe.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Sliced mushrooms
  • Diced onions
  • Grated cheese (the cheesier, the better)
  • Salt and pepper (for seasoning and shenanigans)

Steps

  1. Bake potatoes until they're as tender as a hobbit's heart.
  2. Sauté mushrooms and onions until they're best friends forever (golden brown).
  3. Cut the potatoes in half, scoop out the insides, and mix them with the sautéed goodness. Season with salt and pepper; make it rain seasoning!
  4. Stuff those potato skins like you're preparing them for a cozy hobbit sleepover. Top with a mountain of grated cheese.
  5. Bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly, like a goblin in a hot tub.
