Samwise's Potato Pie

The original hobbit recipe

Layers of potatoes, garlic, and herbs cozy up in a dish, taking a creamy bath with a cheesy rubber ducky.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes (the more, the merrier)
  • Butter
  • Minced garlic (keep those vampires away)
  • Fresh thyme and rosemary (the hobbit's secret garden)
  • Cream
  • Cheese
  • Salt and pepper (seasoning: the spice of hobbit life)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven, because this dish is about to become hotter than the fires of Mount Doom.
  2. Layer those potatoes like you're building a cozy hobbit fort. Butter, garlic, herbs, and a sprinkle of magic (salt and pepper) on each layer.
  3. In a saucepan, make the cream and cheese concoction. Stir until it's as smooth as Legolas' hair.
  4. Pour that creamy goodness over the potato layers.
  5. Bake until the top is golden and the potatoes are tender, like a hobbit in a sunbeam.
